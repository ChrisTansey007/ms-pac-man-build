# Glossary

- **Agent-os**: The workflow system used to coordinate AI workers on this project, where the repository is the source of truth.
- **Blob**: In Pac-Man terminology, the dots that Pac-Man eats for points.
- **Energizer**: The power pellet that allows Pac-Man to eat ghosts for a limited time.
- **Fruit**: Bonus items that appear periodically near the center of the maze for extra points.
- **Ghost House**: The central area where ghosts start and return to after being eaten.
- **Intermission**: The short cartoon-like cutscenes that play between certain levels.
- **Kill Screen**: A level that is impossible to complete due to a bug (not applicable in this faithful recreation).
- **Maze**: The playable area consisting of walls, paths, dots, energizers, and fruit spawn points.
- **Pac-Man**: The player-controlled character that navigates the maze eating dots and avoiding ghosts.
- **Scatter Mode**: A ghost behavior mode where each ghost retreats to its respective corner of the maze.
- **Chase Mode**: A ghost behavior mode where each ghost actively pursues Pac-Man using its unique targeting algorithm.
- **Frightened Mode**: The state of ghosts after Pac-Man eats an energizer, causing them to turn blue and flee.
- **Sprite**: A 2D bitmap used for rendering characters like Pac-Man, ghosts, and fruit.
- **Tick**: A single game update cycle, typically targeting 60 times per second (60fps).
